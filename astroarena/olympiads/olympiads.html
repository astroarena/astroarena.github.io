What are the olympiads?


Back in the ancient times, an Olympiad was a period of four years associated with the Olympic Games of the Ancient Greeks. Nowadays, the term 'olympiad' is used to represent both the original sports competition of the Olympic Games, and an academic tournament, national or international, in different subjects, such as Mathematics, Physics, English and other languages, and also Astronomy and Astrophysics.
